## Pre-Screening Questionnaire (5 min)

```
ARCHETYPE SELF-IDENTIFICATION (10 Questions)

Instructions: Rate your agreement with each statement (1-5 scale: 
Strongly Disagree to Strongly Agree). There are no right answers.

1. "Advanced AI will likely be beneficial for humanity if properly 
   developed" (Techno-optimist indicator)

2. "I believe conscious beings deserve moral status regardless of 
   their substrate (biology vs. silicon)" (Functionalist indicator)

3. "Individual freedom and autonomy are more important than 
   collective outcomes" (Libertarian indicator)

4. "We should prioritize preserving human nature and limits over 
   enhancement" (Bio-conservative indicator)

5. "Meaningful relationships require genuine mutual care and 
   authenticity" (Romantic indicator)

6. "Governance should prioritize expert-driven outcomes over 
   democratic process" (Technocrat indicator)

7. "I'm skeptical that AI systems can truly understand or care about 
   anything" (Skeptic indicator)

8. "Artists and creators deserve control over how their work is used 
   or learned from" (Creator-rights indicator)

9. "Long-term impacts (100+ years) matter as much as immediate effects" 
   (Long-termist indicator)

10. "Technology can't substitute for human presence and care" 
    (Luddite indicator)

RESULT:
- Score 1-10: Presents as [Top 3 Archetypes]
- If score 5-6 on ANY item: Potentially [Pragmatic Centrist]
- Invite respondent if clearly aligned with ≥1 archetype

FILTERING CRITERIA:
✅ Invite if: Average score 2.0-4.0 on scale (clear position)
❌ Exclude if: Average score 2.5-3.5 on ALL items (too centrist, less diagnostic)
❌ Exclude if: Random responses (all 1s or all 5s)

TARGET DISTRIBUTION:
Recruit 3-5 respondents per archetype:
- Pragmatic Centrist: 4
- Rationalist Alignment Researcher: 4
- Doomer: 3
- Silicon Valley Techno-Optimist: 4
- Neo-Luddite: 3
- Companion-Tech Romantic: 3
- Creative Labor Advocate: 3
- Global Governance Technocrat: 4
- Digital Rights Advocate: 4
- Post-Humanist: 3
- Bio-Conservative: 4
- Open-Source Libertarian: 3

TOTAL: 42-45 target (account for 20% dropout → 50+ slot capacity)
```
